# SoviaMate Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
SoviaMate is committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful community. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all contributors and participants to ensure a welcoming and harassment-free experience.

## 2. Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate of others.
- Use inclusive and welcoming language.
- Provide constructive feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Follow all applicable laws and regulations.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments related to race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other characteristic.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information of others without explicit permission.
- Disruptive behavior that interferes with discussions or contributions.

## 4. Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the SoviaMate community at the discretion of the maintainers.

## 5. Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting us at [samson.ailabs@gmail.com](mailto:samson.ailabs@gmail.com). All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

## 6. Acknowledgment
This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and other open-source community guidelines.

By participating in SoviaMate, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct and help create a positive environment for everyone.
